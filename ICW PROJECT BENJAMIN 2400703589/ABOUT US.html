ABOUT US

LifeWater water is a nonprofit organisation bringing clean and safe water to people around the world.

Together, we can end the water crisis

Sice LifeWater was founded in 2006, we've been chasing one ambitious goal, "ending the global water crisis." And while the water crisis is huge, we're optimistic. We know how to solve the problem and we make progress everyday thanks to the help of local partners and generous supporters. If we work together we believe everyone will have access to life's most basic need within our lifetime.
